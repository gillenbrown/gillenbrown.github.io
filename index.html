Astronomy Ph.D. student at the University of Michigan

Research

I use cosmological simulations to study the chemical evolution of galaxies. For my thesis work, I will run high resolution cosmological simulations of a Local Group analog to learn about the evolution of the mass-metallicity relation, metallicity gradients within galaxies, and the chemical content of star clusters.

Outreach and Advocacy

I am an active member of the Michigan Dark Skies group, and have worked to raise awareness of the issue of light pollution and advocate for changes on the local level. I'm also engaged in outreach and science communication to share my love for astronomy with the broader community.
